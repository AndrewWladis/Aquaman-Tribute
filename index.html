Aquaman Tribute

These Are Some Of The Biggest Pieces Of Media In Aquaman's History

  • 1941 - Aquaman first appears in More Fun Comics #73
  • 1960 - Aquaman's sidekick Aqualad first appears in Adventure Comics #269
  • 1963 - Mera (Aquaman's girl friend) first appears in Aquaman #11
  • 1967 - Black Manta first appears in Aquaman #35
  • 1994 - Peter David Starts his legendary run on Aquaman
  • 1998 - Peter David run ends
  • 2011 - A new Aqualad is Intruduced in the comic mini-series Brightest Day and also intruduced in the same year in the hit TV show Young Justice
  • 2012 - Geoff Johns starts his Aquaman run in the DC Comics re-launch The New 52
  • 2013 - The legendery Throne Of Atlantis story arc is finished
  • 2015 - The cartoon of the Throne Of Alantis story arc releases
  • 2018 - The Aquaman movie releases
